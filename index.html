Yvonne Cheng (piano), Julia Foellmer (clarinet) and Tabitha Tuckett (’cello) met in 2017 through UCL Chamber Music Club. They formed Bentham’s Body Ensemble in 2018. Specialising in music for clarinet, ’cello and piano, the ensemble aims to bring Classical music to unusual venues and new audiences. They have performed in the UK and Germany with a repertoire ranging from Beethoven, Brahms and Bruch to Rota and d’Indy. Yvonne studied piano at the Hong Kong Academy Of Performing Arts, Julia was a multiple prize-winner in Germany’s national Young Musician competition, and Tabitha was chosen while a student at Oxford to perform as duo-partner with Nigel Kennedy before specialising in baroque ’cello. At UCL they studied Architecture, Geography and Library Studies respectively. When not being performing musicians, Yvonne has composed music for films, Julia is working on a PhD on soundscapes in the built environment, and Tabitha is a rare-books librarian.
